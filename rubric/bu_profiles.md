## BALCO (Column M)

**Site**: Korba, Chhattisgarh (single integrated complex)

**Capacity / scale**:
- 575 kt cast metal FY24-25 (record 592 kt)
- Two potlines, three casthouses, rolled product plant
- Active expansion: 580→1000 ktpa smelter; 420 ktpa billet plant commissioning FY25-26

**Products**: Aluminium wire rods, ingots, primary foundry alloys, rolled products, **Restora** (low-carbon range)

**Certifications**: ASI Performance Standard V3 (first Indian company)

**Active operational themes** (use these as anchors for ✓ scouting):
- Pot-line monitoring, current efficiency, anode performance
- Casthouse automation (3 casthouses)
- Rolled product expansion (debottlenecking 51→180 ktpa)
- Captive power plant operations
- Biodiesel trial for ladle pre-heating (sustainability angle)
- Biomass co-firing in power boilers (decarbonisation)
- Fly ash utilisation (waste management)
- Korba is in Chhattisgarh = high humidity vs Rajasthan dry climate (matters for some sensor/coating tech)

## VAL-L Lanjigarh (Column N)

**Site**: Lanjigarh, Kalahandi district, Odisha

**Type**: Alumina refinery only (no smelter)

**Capacity**: 5 MTPA (scaled up in 2026 — was 2 MTPA in FY24); target 6 MTPA by FY28
- World's 2nd largest alumina refinery ex-China
- 38% of India's total alumina output
- 155 MW captive power
- Largest single-location alumina refinery in India

**Feeds**: Both Jharsuguda + BALCO smelters

**Active operational themes**:
- Bauxite handling & blending
- Bayer process digesters
- Red mud management & disposal
- Refinery pipelines, slurry transport
- Water recycling (52.52% in FY24-25)
- Captive bauxite mining (Sijimali, OMC sourcing disputes)
- Steam generation, captive power
- Calcination

**Strategic context**: Bauxite supply security is a board-level concern (EGA contract fell through; Sijimali hold-up; OMC pricing dispute).

## VAL-J Jharsuguda (Column O)

**Site**: Jharsuguda, Odisha

**Type**: Aluminium smelter (largest single-location in world)

**Capacity**: 1.85 MTPA (delivered 1.83 Mt FY24-25, 105% of design capacity)

**Power**: 3,615 MW associated thermal CPP (4×600 MW + 9×135 MW units)

**Captive coal blocks**:
- Radhikapur West (6 MTPA) — commissioning FY25-26
- Kuraloi A North (8 MTPA) — commissioning FY25-26
- Ghogharpalli (20 MTPA) — FY26-27

**Active operational themes**:
- Pot-line operations (multiple SEZ + non-SEZ lines)
- Anode plant
- Hot-metal logistics
- Casthouse — new 430 ktpa under commissioning, GAIL natural gas pipeline (62% emissions cut)
- Captive power: coal handling, boiler efficiency, fly ash
- Coal mine commissioning (3 captive blocks live FY26)
- Decarbonisation: pot-lining tech trial (deployed across smelters)
- 59 electric forklifts deployed
- Fly ash utilisation (109%)

**Climate**: Jharsuguda is humid Odisha, not Rajasthan dry — matters for sensor selection.

## ESL (Column P)

**Site**: Bokaro, Jharkhand (integrated steel plant)

**Type**: Integrated long-products steel manufacturer

**Operational chain**: sinter → blast furnace → BOF (basic oxygen furnace) → CCM (continuous casting) → rolling → finishing

**Active operational themes**:
- Sinter plant operations, met-coke supply
- Blast furnace operation
- BOF / hot metal handling
- Continuous casting (CCM)
- Rolling mills (TMT, structurals)
- Finishing line, dispatch, traceability
- Surface defect detection (CRM/HRM)
- Ash & slag handling (cross with Sustainability)
- Captive power
- Discrete manufacturing optimization

**Note**: ESL was a relative late acquisition; integration with broader Vedanta digital roadmap is partial. Engagement Owner often "ESL" rather than Group functions.

## HZL — Hindustan Zinc (Column Q)

**Mines (4 active — all underground, all Rajasthan)**:

| Mine | Location | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rampura Agucha (RAM)** | Bhilwara district, ~230 km N of Udaipur | Underground (transitioned from open-cast) | World's largest zinc mine; 13.4% reserve grade; 4.93 Mtpa; scaling shaft to 11,500 TPD |
| **Sindesar Khurd (SKM)** | Rajsamand, ~65 km NE of Udaipur | Underground | India's largest underground base-metal mine; silver-rich; 5.65 Mtpa |
| **Rajpura Dariba (RDM)** | Rajsamand (same Dariba-Bethumni belt as SKM) | Underground | 1.34 Mtpa; co-located with Dariba Smelting Complex |
| **Zawar Group** | Udaipur | Underground | India's oldest mining complex |

**Smelters / Refineries**:

| Site | Location | Type / capacity |
|---|---|---|
| **Chanderiya Lead-Zinc Smelter (CLZS)** | Chittorgarh, Rajasthan | Pyro 105 kt Zn + 90 kt Pb; Hydro 480 kt Zn — flagship |
| **Dariba Smelting Complex (DSC)** | Rajsamand | Hydro 240 kt Zn |
| **Zinc Smelter Debari (ZSD)** | Udaipur | Hydro 88 kt Zn; new 160 ktpa roaster commissioned FY25 |
| **Pantnagar Metal Plant** | Uttarakhand | Silver refinery (LBMA Good Delivery) + Zn/Pb melting & casting |

**Active operational themes** (HZL has the richest BU pain-point library):
- **Underground mining**: ventilation, paste-fill, shaft hoisting, dewatering, geotech, collision prevention
- **TSF (Tailings Storage Facility) monitoring**: GISTM compliance post-Brumadinho/Samarco — board-level concern
- **Ore beneficiation**: SAG mill, ball mill, flotation circuits, grade analysers
- **RLE (Roast-Leach-Electrowinning) at CLZS**: roaster operations, leaching, cell-house electrolysis
- **ISF (Imperial Smelting Furnace) at CLZS Pyro**: lead-zinc co-recovery
- **Silver refining at Pantnagar**: LBMA chain-of-custody, anti-counterfeit traceability
- **Water management**: 5.5x net water positivity target, 4000 KLD WTP at RAM
- **Energy**: captive thermal + wind + solar
- **Tailings reprocessing**: announced 10 MTPA plant, ₹3,823 Cr investment
- **Digital transformation programme "Drishti"** (SKM): OEE +20% target, equipment tracking, tele-remote operation, central control room

**Strategic themes for P1 – Strategic tagging**:
- GISTM compliance (TSF monitoring tech)
- Decarbonisation & RE storage (HZL is largest power consumer per tonne in group)
- LBMA chain-of-custody (silver, Pantnagar)
- Underground mining digitisation
